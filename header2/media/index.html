So every good story starts with the establishment of Setting. We call Chattanooga, Gig City because this city was the first in the Western Hemisphere to offer 10 Gigabit Connections to all it's Residents and Businesses. This does not include people living in housing, apartments, and mobile homes in a deal to give limited monopolies to the cable companies (one of many examples of Crony Capitalism in Chattanooga, but that discussion is for another time).

The Gigabit connections allowed for home to cloud working environments at an epic scale (that has not been capitalized upon). We have stopped moving high data drives from business to business during the day where people are now relying on file transfer protocol services of various types (see: MassTransit). We have only tapped the biginning of what is possible for this technological environment.
